  • Problem Statement 2: Drone Technology

    PS (Prelims): Developing heavy lift drone which will be able to deliver relief material, food packets & other things in a disaster hit area or remote area at the earliest. Can be helpful in emergency situations. Students can either give computer simulation regarding the load capacity or a physical model.

    PS (Mains) Extension: Developing drone jammers which could block and work on interfering with GPS, Radio signals for the same. It should make the drone redundant. Identification of drone control station overlapping drone camera images on goggle maps pinpoint delivery of packages.

    Expectation: Deployment of Drone which could lift heavy weights along with the ability to dismantle any surveillance drone with jammers. Also, drone could be integrated with google with pinpoint accuracy and mapping of device.

  • Problem Statement 3: Implementation of AI/ML in the Field of Education

    PS (Prelims): Today our education system faces a lot of issues like lack of proper transportation to school, basic infrastructure, qualified teachers, quality of education, low awareness of importance of education, etc are just a small portion of challenges which holds back the development. Develop an AI/ML model which can tackle these problems and improvise the education system.

    Expectation: Create an AI/ML solution that addresses the mentioned challenges in the education system and brings about improvements.

It creates an unhygienic condition for the people and creates a bad smell around the surroundings that leads to the spread of some deadly diseases & human illness.

    PS (Mains) Extension: [Add the extension description for the main round here, if applicable]

    Expectation: Design an "IoT Based Waste Management for Smart Cities" which can overcome these problems.

  • Problem Statement 5: Dark Web/Dark Net

    PS (Prelims): Tool that could search for keywords across dark net search engines, along with an alerting mechanism. It must be a simple interface/ Console which must connect to VPN and then help to find the details rather than police searching for websites, links, platforms on dark web. It must also be able to fetch data from different browsers like TOR, TAILS, I2P, Freenet etc. Participants must develop a simple console which automatically latched to a VPN and then connect through Dark Net Browser/Deep Net browser. And then it should be able to filter data based upon keyword, geo location, state, district, date

    PS (Mains) Extension: Expose IP details of dark net sites & users. Tool which could identify a seller of any product and provide ways to track down the person. It should be able to able to gather/crawl details about all websites, chat rooms, shopping sites & other information related to dark web. It should be a handy intelligence and data collection tool on the dark web.

    Expectation: A handy tool for dark web intelligence which could help the police in tracking down the websites, gather intelligence and also trace the person, groups involved in any illegal activity. Tool must be able to perform analysis of the gathered information.

  • Problem Statement 6: Traffic/Crowd Management

    PS (Prelims): Devising smart traffic management system. A real-time console to be developed which will intimate regarding availability of hotel rooms, Heli services availability, other facilities & total people present at Shri Kedarnath Complex. (RFID)/ QR code scanner for real-time check.

    PS (Mains) Extension: Console to track parking space, traffic jams, AI for alert regarding crowding and how it could be integrated with Google Maps. RFID tagged barrier can be installed to monitor the traffic, number of cars passed, number of people crossed. Even QR code scan technology can also be integrated. All this data to get integrated at a centralized database at district level for better monitoring. Smart signal controller for scheduling signals for a route with congestion reduction and traffic management and route diversion.

    Expectation: Real-time display of data showing availability for rooms, parking, helicopter service, display of traffic situation- Road jam, parking space etc. this problem is regarding smart tourist management in Kedarnath and other Char Dharm sites

  • Problem Statement 7: News Alert/Flash

    PS (Prelims): A simple software that includes all major departments dealing with situations like accidents, fire, etc so that once the incident has occurred the concerned departments get details and hence work more efficiently saving time. E.g., somebody reports a fire incident or accident incident then the report must immediately go to the concerning district or police station. These social media tweet text have been mined so as to identify the complaints regarding various road transportation issues of traffic, accident, potholes, garbage, and pollution.

    PS (Mains) Extension: The tool should work on a flash mechanism method. The accident occurrence should be flashed on screens of concerned authorities. Also, feedback or response action should be timely fed by the concerned. Centralized database to be made all social media accounts/pages are integrated and tracking of complaints can be done. Also, the need for geotagging the place and generating hotspot for specific time of complaint. E.g., one area report for maximum chain snatching complaints. Analytical analysis of social media posts for sentiment prediction. Database of regular habitual offenders on social media Identified profiles-based web crawlers for content and photos

    Expectation: A mini demonstration from the side of the participants on how news will be flashed, response time to be showed and then a summary report to be generated having basic details.

  • Problem Statement 8: Road Safety

    PS (Prelims): Smart Pillars or sensors which may use Radio waves to measure the speed of upcoming vehicles and generate the centralized data for over speeding of vehicles. Can be used for automatic Challan for violation. Must develop 04 such pillars for problem extension in the main round.

    PS (Mains) Extension: Vehicle honk system where Sensors/Smart Pillars will interact with each other, and vehicles will be told about approaching vehicles from the other blind fold end or U-turn in hills. For instance- in a hilly area with a blind curve, both directions vehicle can’t see or gauge whether another vehicle coming in their direction with speed, such sensors based upon speed instantly generate an alert/honking system to alert both drivers

    Expectation: Road Safety is a big concern for police and such smart sensors/smart pillars will help to reduce accidents. Will be a useful installation where such sensors will alert in both directions regarding the movement of vehicles along with measurement of Speed.

  • Problem Statement 9: Intelligence

    PS (Prelims): Tool which can horizontally crawl and gather data across different platforms. This search could be based upon mail ID or the mobile number.

    PS (Mains) Extension: The information is gathered vertically too where details from the deep web and dark web can be found related to a mobile number or email ID.

    Expectation: Police will be able to crowd source the information from different platforms and instantly develop the profile of the suspected person. E.g., based upon different types of classification.
